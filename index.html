Our Features

Our system enables the digitalization and visualization of contract management and execution in real-time between stakeholders.

Contract Structure & Process

Model and track contract execution processes, ensuring adherence to contractual obligations.

Blockchain-based Smart Contracts

Utilize blockchain technology to automate contract execution, enhancing security and traceability.

Real-time Process Visualization

Gain a comprehensive overview of contract status and progress, enabling informed decision-making.

Seamless Data Integrations

Integrate with BIM (3D view) and CDE (off-chain storage) for a holistic data management approach.
